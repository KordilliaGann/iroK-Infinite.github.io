Finally got to my C Project

Date 3/28/2015 Author Kori  | Category : Computers

 

For the past few weeks I have been working on this computer programming book called Head First C. This week I finally got deep enough into it that I reached the first project. The project called for me to make a plant dehydration alarm. If the plant's soil was not moist enough it would shout, "Feed Me" and the LED on my Arduino would be lit . Then, when it was wet enough, it would say, "Thank you Seymour" (if you don't get that reference it's the plant from Little Shop of Horrors). It took me two hours but I finally got it to work. I did need to do a little research because using just normal C and Arduino's C is different. After a little trial and error I was able to get the program working just like it was supposed to!
