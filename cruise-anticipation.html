Cruise Anticipation

Date 2/28/2015 Author Kori  | Category : Life

 

This week has been really long. So far I've watched babies driven tons of hours, and learned a lot of subjects. For my current four hours a day I've been taking free online courses about Game Theory, AI, Algorithms, and in case I finish one of those early Java. My dad also found this amazingly good book about C, so I've been learning that as well. Anyways tomorrow my parents and I are heading to some city to go on a cruise. Awesome, more driving, but it's totally worth it to get a cruise as a last hurrah before three years of no vacations. So that's all I have done this week and I put on my grit sheet, that I made with the CSIT-In-3 that I would make a blog each week, so you can expect one next week.
