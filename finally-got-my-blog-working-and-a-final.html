Finally got my Blog Working and a Final

Date 5/22/2015 Author Kori  | Category : Computers

 

Today I got my computer to work turns out that if you move files to your computer the things that make them run won't be there as well. So today I downloaded everything that makes my blog work and changed my old path from my old computer to my new path from this one. After I did all that my blog finally worked, the only thing is now I have to add sudo to everything. Onto an off topic subject I took my one Final yesterday in math. As you may or may not know I help a girl in my class with math, so for my final act of helping I made her notes for the test because she was freaking out about all her finals. Since I spent the night before the test staring at our previous tests and trying to explain my thinking on paper for her I had a super easy time on the Final, I was actually suprised when I finsihed because I thought it would be at least a little harder. That should prove that as long as you study life will be fine I have never understood why people get super stressed before Finals. I ended up memorizing everything and not using any notes even though I could've.
