Tomorrow Marks the Beginning of the Epic Adventure of Battlecode

Date 1/4/2015 Author Kori  | Category : Battlecode

 

After much convincing, by annoying him to death, I was able to convince Angel to join me on our team 2B|!2B (to be or not to be). So, starting tomorrow we will begin our awesome journey of making cooperative robots in Battlecode. Since this is our first year we're not really expecting a lot, it would be a major achievement to just have the navigation working, since it appears to be super hard. Winning against anybody would be super awesome but it is highly unlikely.

The things I hope to get out of Battlecode are:

1) Being able to successfully use GitHub with another person on the same project. 2) Becoming more familiar with Java. 3) Learning awesome tactics from more experienced teams. 4) Getting used to Battlecode so that it will be easier next year.
