New Computers and Downside of Advanced Technology

Date 5/8/2015 Author Kori  | Category : Computers

 

So, I recently got a new computer. It's a really awesome laptop with sixteen gigabytes of RAM and a terabyte of memory, it even has a NVDIA graphics card with four gigs of GRAM. My Lenovo laptop, is also dual bootable if you have a wireless adapter USB. Yesterday, I dual booted my computer for the second time but this time all by myself (the first time my dad helped me). Dual booting is amazingly easy all you do is set aside some space, download ubuntu on a USB, then turn it off holding shift and press install ubuntu. For better instructions you can use the same site I did Dual Booting Alongside Windows. I totally suggest dual booting it's really easy and seems a lot better than using a virtual machine.
