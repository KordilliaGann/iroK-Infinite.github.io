Women in Robotics

Date 5/1/2015 Author Kori  | Category : Computers

 

This week I went to the SVR Influencer Series: Women in Robotics event. It turned out not to be what I expected. The event was question led so most people just asked how to get a job in robotics. The answer to that question is have a really awesome github profile and to have lots of projects. I also learned that currently roboticists are experiencing a shortage in software developers. I'm somewhat hoping that still stands in three years. On the positive side before the meeting, I was talking to this really nice person who offered to help me get an internship in her company. She worked for the Other Machine Company. Unfortunately, I only got her first name, so I have also learned to always get people's last names as well.
